Great Steampunk Adventure

⭐ 9/10
19 November 2021

In the cities of Piltover and Zaun there is unrest, Piltover the upper city literally looks down on and oppresses Zaun. We have gangs of thieves, rebels, steampunk Stormtroopers, magic melded with science. Also a tale of two sisters falling out and Scientist/Mages battling against the conservatism of the Council who fear the unleashing of uncontrollable powers.

Visually stunning

⭐ 9/10
22 November 2021

Made by gamers (RIOT) for gamers, but, also, is a stand alone piece of art for people, like me, unfamiliar with the game, League of Legends. The series is visually stunning in 3D and 2D animation with beautiful attention to detail. There is incredible world building, an engaging story line, music that serves as narration and a stellar voice cast. I give this series a 9 (superb) out of 10.

Best Animated series in a long time

⭐ 9/10
27 February 2022

Arcane is a Netflix animated series based on the video game League of Legends. I am not familiar with this game but the series is one of a kind. The animation, storytelling, the sound effects are one of a kind. The 2D and 3D dimensional artistry brought the chapters to life. The compelling story shows the harsh reality and struggles between the rich and the poor.
