Satisfy Your Sweet Cravings with Fudgy Brownies!

Indulge in the ultimate chocolate delight with our fudgy brownie recipe. These decadent treats boast a rich, moist texture and intense chocolate flavor that will satisfy even the most discerning sweet tooth. With a simple mix of ingredients, they're quick to make and impossible to resist.

Treat Yourself!

Prep Time

15 min.

Cook Time

30 min.

Total Time

45 min.

Servings

24

Ingredients

  • 20 tbsp butter, plus more, softened, for greasing
  • 8 oz good-quality semisweet chocolate (225 g), roughly chopped
  • ½ cup dark brown sugar (110 g), packed
  • 2 cups granulated sugar (400 g)
  • 6 large eggs
  • ¾ cup unsweetened dutch process cocoa powder (90 g), divided
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (125 g)
  • 1 tablespoon espresso powder
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • flaky sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a 9x13-inch dark metal pan with butter, then line with parchment paper. Grease parchment with butter.
  2. Combine the chopped chocolate, ¼th cup cocoa powder, and espresso powder in a heatproof medium bowl and set aside.
  3. Add butter to a saucepan over medium heat and cook for 5 minutes. Immediately pour the butter over the chocolate mixture and whisk until completely melted, then set aside.
  4. Beat the sugars, vanilla extract, salt, and eggs in a large bowl, with an electric hand mixer on high speed for about 10 minutes.
  5. With the mixer on, pour in the chocolate mixture and blend until smooth.
  6. Sift in the flour and remaining cocoa powder. Use a spatula to gently fold until combined.
  7. Pour the batter into the baking pan and smooth it with a spatula. Bake for about 20 minutes.
  8. Remove the baking pan from the oven, then lightly tap on a flat surface 1-2 times. Sprinkle with salt.
  9. Return the pan to the oven and bake until a wooden skewer inserted into the brownies' center comes out fudgy but the edges look cooked through, about 20 minutes more.
  10. Use the parchment paper sides to lift the brownies out of the pan. Cut into 24 bars and serve immediately.

Tips

For sweeter brownies, use 1 3/4 cups of sugar.

Use stevia instead of sugar for a healthier option.

Add chocolate chunks for an extra burst of chocolatey goodness.

Add nuts or other toppings for added flavor or texture.

Adjust the cooking time based on the size of your baking pan.
